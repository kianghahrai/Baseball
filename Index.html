What is baseball?

Baseball is a game played with a bat,a ball, and gloves between two teams of 9 players. Teams alternate as batters and fielders, exchanging sides when 3 members of the batting team is put out.

History

The origin of baseball dates back to the 18th century in England, where men played a game similiar to baseball using scraps for equipment. The game was brought by immigrants to North America, where more modernized versions of baseball developed. The first baseball ball club was formed in the 1830's. As the clubs rose in popularity, the first professional leagues arrived in the 1870's. By the late 19th century, baseball was recognized as the national sport of the United States

What are positions?

There are 9 different positions in baseball.They all serve different purposes. The Left,Right,and Center Fielders try and cover all the balls that go far and into the outfield. The Catcher and Pitcher go together as they communicate together to try and get the batter out. Lastly, the shortstop, third baseman, first baseman, and second baseman cover the balls that are hit in the infield.
